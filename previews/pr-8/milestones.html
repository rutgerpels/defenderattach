Methodology

Account-level gap: account has Migration milestones but no Defender for Cloud milestones.

Attached account: account appears in both workbooks.

Opportunity-level gap: for attached accounts, a Migration Opportunity ID has no Defender milestone with the same account and Opportunity ID.

Priority: HIGH = SalesStageName is Inspire & Design or Listen and Consult; MEDIUM = other known sales stage or valid workload; LOW = no target sales stage and unclear workload. Rows are then sorted by the earliest due date.
